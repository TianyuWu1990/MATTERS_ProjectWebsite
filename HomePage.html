What is MATTERS?

The Massachusetts Technology, Talent, and Economic, Reporting System (MATTERS) is a collaborative project between Worcester Polytechnic Institute and the Massachusetts High Technology Council (MHTC) whose mission is to make Massachusetts the world's most attractive place in which to create and grow a high technology business. The system is designed to measure Massachusetts economic competitiveness compared to other states over time.

A number of WPI undergrad and graduate students have worked on this project. The website is an online analytics dashboard where users can view economic, educational, and demographic data from many different public sites. The data can be viewed with descriptive visual analytics. The website also incorporates a suite of tools for adding new data integration pipelines through an administration center.
